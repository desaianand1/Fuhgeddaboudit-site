Why?

Remember late 2022? ChatGPT took the internet, media, and tech communities by storm. It was difficult to engage in any activity, read an article, or have a conversation without encountering the term "AI" or some related buzzword.

AI-related jargon infiltrated nearly every medium I used. I just wanted a break from this deluge, so I made this project over an April Fool's weekend.

This tool may be whimsical, but it has helped ease my mind. I can now browse Linkedin in peace again.

Hopefully, it can provide some respite for you too ❤️!
