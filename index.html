Why?

In late 2022, ChatGPT became a sensation across the internet, media, and tech circles. It was difficult to engage in any activity, read an article, or have a conversation without encountering the term "AI" or some related buzzword.

AI-related jargon infiltrated my workplace, home, and even my interactions with friends. I just wanted a break from this deluge, so I made this project over an April Fool's weekend.

This really helped me escape some of that noise in a whimsical way. I can now browse Linkedin in peace again.
