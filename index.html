Fuhgeddaboudit

Why?

When ChatGPT launched in late 2022, it quickly blew up all over media. Within a few weeks, nearly every piece of media I consumed, in any form, mentioned ChatGPT, GPT-4, AI or some other related jargon. I was saturated and frustrated, my tech career and colleagues further compounding on this word-assault. Well, to alleviate my burden (and hopefully yours) I have created this tool. It's pretty whimsical, but I think the Web needs more of that nowadays :)
