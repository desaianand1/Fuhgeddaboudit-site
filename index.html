Why?

When ChatGPT launched in late 2022, it quickly blew up all over digital media. Within a few weeks, nearly every piece of media I consumed, regardless of form, volleyed a barrage of AI-related jargon at me. Chat-GPT, AI, GPT-4, AutoGPT, Transformers, Midjourney, DALL-E, Large Language Models, Deep Learning...the list seemed evergrowing

With this word-assault came a wave of fear-mongering, paranoia and AI saturation. I could not escape it at work, home or during lesiure, so I made this extension over April's Fools day as a mental escape.

Over time, I learned that I was not alone in my dread or frustration. Turns out AI Anxiety is very real and is likely to get worse as AI news proliferates.
